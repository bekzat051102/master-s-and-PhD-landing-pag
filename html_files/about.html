about us

Ala-Too International University is dedicated to educating and empowering our students to become professionals and leaders of their communities in Central Asia and in the world.

In 2013, AIU opened the IT&Business College, and then the Distance Learning Center, master's and postgraduate courses, which expanded the prospects for new growth and development of the university. Today AIU has 4 academic faculties, 19 undergraduate programs, 2 bachelor's degree programs, 2 master's degree programs, 10 master's programs, 6 postgraduate programs, 3 doctoral / Ph.D programs and 3 institutes.

4 Faculties:

-Faculty of Engineering and Informatics;
-Faculty of Humanities;
-Faculty of Economics and Management;
-Faculty of Medicine.
